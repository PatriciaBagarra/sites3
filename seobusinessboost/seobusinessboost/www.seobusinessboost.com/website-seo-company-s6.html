3 SEO Trends Your Website Must Be Prepared for:

There are 3 rising SEO trends that you should prepare your website for: Google Featured Snippets, Position Zero, and Voice Search. Each of these new strategies will make a big difference to your online marketing strategies. Here's what you need to know so you can prepare your website to win the top spot on search engines.

How to Optimize for Google's Featured Snippets:

Featured snippets are the select search results that appears at the top of Google search results. It aims to directly answer a question while giving the benefit of more exposure.

Optimizing for Long-Tail Keyword Terms:

Start with keyword research and optimize long-tail terms that users are likely to search for. Work with it on your on-page SEO by answering them in the body of your content. You can use multiple question keywords per article to gain more chances of being featured.

Professionally Adding Schema Mark-Up:

Another step is adding Schema mark-up protocols to your website. Make sure your snippets are 45-97 words as this is the golden standard for featured snippets.
