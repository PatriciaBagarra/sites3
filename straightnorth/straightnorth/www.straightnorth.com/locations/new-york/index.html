New York Marketing Agency

Founded in 1997, Straight North is a New York marketing company that works with small and midsized firms to help them build bigger sales lead pipelines. In a market as sophisticated and competitive as New York, it takes a great deal of experience and know-how to get results.

We have both.

Capabilities of Our New York Marketing Agency

With online and offline marketing options, we stay focused on lead generation at all times. In the end, no matter what type of marketing you’re doing, it boils down to lead generation and revenue — all the website traffic and fancy brochures in the world won’t matter unless prospects walk through the real or virtual door. Our capabilities include:

  • Keyword research. Our keyword selection process looks at keyword volume, buying intent, relevance, competitiveness, and other factors. Without solid keyword selection, campaigns will be shaky no matter how creative or energetic.
  • Design. Our web and graphic designers blend creativity and adherence to best practices for user experience and conversion optimization to produce landing pages, email templates, brochures, slide presentations, and other items that grab the prospect’s attention and influence them to buy.
  • Copywriting. Our New York marketing firm has a team of writers and editors experienced in writing for a wide range of industries, enabling them to create content that establishes your credibility and conveys your value proposition in words your prospects will understand.
  • Marketing specialists. Your SEO, PPC, email, display advertising and/or retargeting campaign will be managed by an expert in the field, a person with up-to-date knowledge of best practices in every aspect of the work.
  • Strategists. While marketing campaigns involve a multitude of details, all the work is in vain without a solid strategic framework. Our leadership and strategy teams listen carefully to your marketing and general business requirements, and gain an understanding of your value proposition, customers and competitors to help guide the development of all creative work used in your campaign.
  • Account managers. Your entire campaign will be coordinated by a dedicated account manager responsible for communication and keeping the campaign flowing and continually improving. We want your input and feedback along the way so that none of your ideas or concerns gets lost in the shuffle.

Whether your market is New York City, the New York region or the U.S., we can create a campaign that reaches your target audience and generates phone, website form and face-to-face inquiries.
