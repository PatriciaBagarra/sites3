Our Accreditations

At Xperience we are immensely proud of what we do. We are committed to our customers and put them at the heart of everything we do.

Industries

Excellence Comes as Standard

We fuel our clients success with the latest technology, helping them to accelerate profitability and position for growth. Our accreditations demonstrate our digital transformation capabilities and prove we are a credible, reliable and trusted partner.

Find Out More About Us

Sage Certified Partner

This accolade recognises a group of Sage approved partners. It demonstrates we continually seek to improve our customer services, demonstrating our ongoing commitment to our customers.

Sage Developer Programme

As a Sage Developers’ Programme member, we have access to a comprehensive range of development tools to help us develop, support and integrate Sage products to meet the varying needs of our clients.

ISO 27001 :2013

The Cloud Simplified is certified to the ISO 27001: 2013, the international standard for information security management system. It demonstrates we follow best practice in information security and risk management, from a legal, physical and technical perspective.

Learn more

Swiftpage Diamond Partner

We are one of just 4 UK partners to receive the Diamond Partner status from Swiftpage, assuring you we are a credible and trusted partner. The accolade acknowledges our 19 year track record as a CRM provider and the experience and skill of our people.

International Act Partner of the Year

We are the #1 Act! International Business Partner of the Year. Since 2002 we have won the Act! Partner of the Year award 12 times, recognising our commitment to delivering customer service excellence.

RIPE NCC Member

We are a member of RIPE NCC which provides Internet resource allocations, registration services and coordination activities to support the operation of the Internet globally.

Nominet Accredited Partner

Channel Partner status is an exclusive merit recognising UK domain registrars who offer premium services. In attaining this, we have shown exceptional data quality standards and service excellence.

Sophos Gold Partner

As Sophos Gold Partner, we offer next-gen cybersecurity services and tools, ensuring your systems and data are safe from biggest cyber threats.

The Hellios Financial Supplier

Xperience is committed to responsible business practices that meet regulatory requirements and compliance standards.
In order to demonstrate our commitment and credentials in servicing the financial sector, we are proud to announce that we have become fully registered on the FSQS supplier qualification system.

Learn more

Crown Commercial Service Supplier

Xperience has been named as an approved supplier for G-Cloud 12, the Crown Commercial Service’s online marketplace for the public sector. We’ll be focusing on delivering Cloud Hosting and Cloud Software solution, including Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Patch Management as a Service (PMaaS) and Security as a Service (SECaaS).
