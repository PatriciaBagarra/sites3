Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central

A complete ERP solution, previously known as Dynamics NAV, designed to give you greater visibility and control of your key business data to drive business growth.

What is Dynamics 365 Business Central?

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is an all-in-one ERP platform allowing you to view real-time insights in one place, removing manual reporting, siloed data and multiple excel sheets. Connecting many of the Microsoft applications including Microsoft 365 (formerly Office 365), Business Central ensures data is at the centre of your organisation streamlining your processes and allowing you to make informed business decisions.

Why Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central?

With Business Central you can:

  • Move away from data silos and bring all your key business information into a single platform, from Core Financials through to Operations and even CRM, allowing you to manage your business processes from start to finish, all in one place.
  • Work smarter and more efficiently with real-time business information and processes directly available from the platform you work in every day….your email!
  • Stop reporting in the past and start reporting in the present and most importantly looking into the future. Business Central brings live, finger on the pulse, business reporting and analytics directly to each user
  • Don’t worry about the “what happens if” and be safe in the knowledge that Business Central is a scalable solution allowing you to add more applications when you need it to help you on your digital transformation journey.
