Reliable Backups for Reliable Recovery

Data is the new oil. Today’s business rely heavily on the data that they store. Whether it’s operational, machine, or big data, businesses now need to drive their strategy based on this information. However, collecting and storing data is no longer enough and can pose a significant risk to the business if you do not have the appropriate systems and processes in place to retrieve and recover data. An event such as software failure, data corruption, or a human-caused event, such as a malicious attack (virus or malware) would have a large impact on profitability if data can’t be retrieved. And this is where reliable backups become indispensable.

Backup your Microsoft 365 with Xperience

Protect yourself from accidental deletion or security threats by backing up your Microsoft 365.

Microsoft 365 (formerly know has Office 365) has transformed how businesses think about and use cloud. It is a cloud-based productivity suite combining familiar Office applications with a range of collaboration tools to help your organisation improve productivity, remain agile and competitive. The Microsoft 365 suite enables employees to work at any time and from any place.

However, with remote working a necessity for most businesses and with a rise in cyberattacks, it has never been more important that companies take the right steps to protect their data.

At Xperience we have the appropriate systems and processes to retrieve and recover data. An event such as software failure, data corruption, or a human-caused event like accidental deletion, or a malicious attack (virus or malware) would have a large impact on profitability if data can’t be retrieved. And this is where reliable backups become indispensable.

Xperience’s Cloud Backup was made for this purpose. It will back up all of your Microsoft 365 data on Exchange, SharePoint, OneDrive, Microsoft Teams etc, ensuring your data is protected.

 

Why should I back up Microsoft 365 data?
  • Accidental deletion – Be aware of a hard delete when an item is  unrecoverable.
  • Retention policy gaps – Retention policies change and are difficult to keep up with, and Office 365 has limited backup and retention policies.
  • Internal security threats – Microsoft doesn’t know the difference between a regular user and a terminated employee attempting to delete data.
  • External security threats – Including malware and viruses, like ransomware, that are an ongoing business threat.
  • Legal and compliance requirements – Easily retrieve emails, files or other data amid legal action.
  • Hybrid email deployments – Good backup solutions protect your data across on-premises Exchange and Office 365 Exchange Online.
  • Teams’ data structure – Purpose-built backup solution can protect  data across Teams and associated apps, like One Drive.
