A solution for a growing business

Xperience has over 15 years’ experience implementing Sage solutions, working with over 230+ clients across the UK. With highly experienced and accredited experts who have in-depth knowledge of Sage 200, you can be assured that you are in safe hands.

Sage 200 gives you the ability to manage your business on the move. This is where the power of desktop meets the freedom of the cloud, providing real-time business insights and data integration whenever and wherever you are. If you want to accelerate your digital transformation journey, integrating Sage 200 with Microsoft 365 (previously Office 365), a powerful productivity suite, means that you can begin this journey at the heart of your business – accessing real-time reports and customer insights, and automating approvals directly from your inbox via Outlook integration.

It’s the ideal solution for companies with a turnover of £1 to £50 million, or typically 10 to 200 employees, across all business sectors. This makes Sage 200 a highly accessible and flexible platform.

Which Sage 200 Package is right for you?

Sage 200 is a powerful business management solution – it comes in two levels, choose from Standard and Professional based on your needs.

 

Sage 200 StandardSage 200 Professional
The standard package offers simplicity for businesses with straightforward requirements. It provides core financials and stock capabilities for deeper insights to power your business growth.For businesses with more complex processes, Sage 200 Professional provides a fully featured ERP solution. Manage everything from financials to inventory and customer relationships.
