Website Monitoring

You'll be notified by email or text message whenever your site is down or unreachable, making it easy for you to get it up and running as soon as possible. Reliable uptime is critical to having a high-ranking website, so you'll find this tool particularly useful for maintaining a steady traffic stream.

Track Your Site's Loading Speed

The SEO ToolBox allows you to automatically monitor your site's loading speed. That means you'll be able to pinpoint when your server is having speed issues – and correct this. Again, this will result in more traffic for your site.

Easy Sitemap Generator

Input your site's URL. Click a button. With the SEO ToolBox, that's all it takes to get an XML sitemap of your website. And sitemaps are important to SEO – submitting a sitemap to Google Webmaster Tools is one way to rank higher on search engines.

Detect Broken Links

The SEO ToolBox will scan your site for broken links and other broken resources – such as images, CSS files, and JavaScript files. You can then quickly repair these broken links and resources, making your website a lot friendlier to visitors and search engines alike.
