Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Clerk.io for free?
Yes - You can test out Clerk.io for free for as long as you like. Our only requirements are that you work actively on your account and you are not using Clerk.io in production. By default, all new accounts expire after 7 days. We can do longer trials that fit your needs but only if you follow an agreed upon implementation roadmap.
How does the Return On Investment guarantee work?
We know that Clerk.io works - That's why we are willing to give you a guaranteed 10x ROI. If we haven't achieved that after one year of using Clerk.io correctly we will refund you the amount needed to achieve a 10x ROI. You can qualify by writing to our support team and have them confirm that you are using Clerk.io correctly. From the date of confirmation, you will be qualified - as long as you don't make any major changes to your Clerk.io setup. If you do that, you will need to be qualified again. That's it, simple as it should be.
How does Clerk.io measure usage?
Not all API requests are of equal value - so why should you pay the same for each? At Clerk.io we try to align our pricing with the actual value we create for you. For most use cases "1 API request = 1 usage" but some simple API requests are only counted as 0.5 or 0.2 in usage. Some requests like product updates and 0-results are always completely free. Thus your usage is always lower than the total amount of API requests you make to us.
Where can I see my usage?
You can at any time see your historic and predicted usage at your companys Usage Dashboard on my.clerk.io. We store all historic usage so you have a complete account on how it has developed and we also forecast your current monthly usage so there is full transparency.
How can I know if Clerk.io is performing?
From day one, you will have access to our dashboard, which drills down not only how much is sold through Clerk.io, but also how we affect your key KPIs, such as basket size, avg. order value and conversion rate.
Before you start we can even give you a full demo of how Clerk.io will perform on your won data so you can compare Clerk.io side-by-side with your existing setup.
Can I run multiple stores?
Yes. Your plan is based on the combined usage on all of your sites. We don't charge you for having additional sites. Actually, it's beneficial, since you get a lower unit cost price.
Can I use my own design?
Yes. Results from Clerk.io can be styled just the way you like and integrated in any way you like. When rendered they are just as much a part of your site as any other element.
Can I get a development environment?
Yes. In fact, many of our clients do that. You can simply use a store as a development environment. That way you can test anything before releasing it to your live store.
What if I go over my plan limits?
With us you just pay for what you use so your pricing will follow your usage. That's it. Your shop will continue to function as usual, you will still be able to access your account and all your data will still be available to you.
Do I need to pay extra for premium support?
No! All plans come with full 5-star support! You can contact us by chat, email, phone within support hours which is Monday to Friday 09:00 - 17:00 CET. We'll pick up the phone when you call and answer email or chat right away.
